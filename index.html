Raw Materials

Raw materials are the basic materials from which goods and products are made. Usually, raw materials are natural resources,or substances examples are: oil, iron and wood are all common raw materials used in the production of goods which are processed to form a final product.

Manufacturing

The production of merchandise for use or sale using labour, machines, tools, chemical and biological processing. The term manufacturing may reference to a range of human activity,but this is most commonly applied to industrial production, in which raw materials are transformed into finished goods on a large scale.

Transport

Is the movement of people, animals and goods between locations. Modes of transport include air, rail, road, water, cable, pipeline and space. The field can be divided into infrastructure, vehicles and operations. Transport is important because it enables trade between persons, which is essential for the development of the world.
