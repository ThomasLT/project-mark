Fuel efficient driving

Adopt these five fuel-efficient driving techniques to reduce your fuel consumption and carbon dioxide emissions by as much as 25 percent:

  1. Accelerate gently
  2. Maintain a steady speed
  3. Anticipate traffic
  4. Avoid high speeds
  5. Coast to decelerate

Fuel-efficient driving can not only save you hundreds of pounds in fuel each year but also improve road safety and prevent unnecessary wear on your vehicle.
